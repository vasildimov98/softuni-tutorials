Unit Testing

1. Seven Testing Principles
A. Testing is contest dependent
B. Exhaustive testing is impossible
We don't have to test everything exept trivial cases - depends on time and priority!
C. Early testing is always preferred
D. Defect clustering - a small number of modules usually contains most of the defects discover
E. Pesticide Paradox- testing should be on going process
F. Testing shows presence of defects
G. Absence of errors fallacy
